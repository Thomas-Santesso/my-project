Geometry

Geometry is an area of ​​mathematics that deals with shapes, sizes, distances and properties of space. Includes the study of points, lines, surfaces, solids and their properties and relationships.

From Euclidean geometry, which focuses on flat, solid shapes, to non-Euclidean geometry, which explores curved and complex spaces, geometry is fundamental to understanding the world around us. Applications of geometry include engineering, architecture, physics, computer graphics, and more.

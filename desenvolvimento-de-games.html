Game Development

Game development involves the creation of electronic games, ranging from planning and design to programming and testing. It is an interdisciplinary area that combines artistic and technical skills.

Knowledge in game development includes character design, narrative, game mechanics, and the use of game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine. This area is ideal for anyone who has an innovative idea for a game.

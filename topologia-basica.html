Basic Topology

Topology is an area of ​​mathematics that studies the properties of spaces that are preserved through continuous deformations, such as stretching and twisting, but not tears or gluing.

Basic topology covers fundamental concepts such as topological spaces, continuity, compactness, connectedness and homeomorphisms. It is essential for understanding many other areas of pure and applied mathematics.
